How to use?
Edit your quick settings menu.

Add icon to your toolbar.

Sometimes ambient light sensor can be annoying in environments with lots of changing light sources or moving shadows.
Selecting it will switch on or off ambient light sensor.

Long press will bring up brightness slider (you can change this behaviour using toggles above). You can toggle light sensor by pressing the round button.
